Android and iOS App

The mobile app is currently available on the Google Play Store. The app comes with locations that signs are posted at and also allows the user to add their own locations as well as favorite locations. The app asks the user to log in through facebook or coninue as an anonymous user without the ability to favorite or add locations. The app takes only the name, profile picture, and email from the user's facebook and never posts to their wall or sends emails to their account.

The app comes with standard locations where signs are or will be posted. Users who log in through their facebooks can add their own locations, such as home or work, so that they can receive information quickly. Added locations will be displayed in the user's profile and the list of all locations. Users can also favorite locations, default and added, and these will be added to the favorites list on the location page.

The iOS app is currently in development. Although the team does not have a release date determined, they predict that it will be available by 2016.
