Contact See[k] and Design for America

See[k] is one of many projects in progress in the Virginia Tech branch of Design for America, an organization focused on service through design. It is comprised of multi-disciplinary students of every academic year. Students come together to pursue projects about any topic of interest to members. DFA also takes on sponsored projects to assist local companies and organizations, such as Corning and Chrysler who have worked with DFA in the past 2 years. Past projects have also covered the topics of interesting children in STEM topics, helping the elderly remember to take prescriptions, helping students with disabilities stay informed on accessabiltiy, and more.
