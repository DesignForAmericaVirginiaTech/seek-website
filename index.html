What is See[k]?

See[k] is a project that began in 2012 with the goal of encouraging alternative forms of transportation for the health of both the user and the planet. See[k] uses a mobile app and signs placed around the Virginia Tech campus and the Town of Blacksburg to provide information about travel time. Both the physical signs and the mobile app provide estimated travel times for walking, biking, bussing, and driving.

Although signs are not currently posted, the team is in the process of printing the signs and gaining the proper permissions. The iOS app is also currently is production. The team predicts that both of these will be available by the end of the year so be sure to keep an eye out for them!
