Signs in Blacksburg

Although signs are not currently posted, the team is currently developing and printing signs to provide information on travel time by walking, biking, bussing through Blacksburg Transit, and driving from pre determined locations. An example of these signs can be seen below. The signs are designed to direct students, faculty, and residents of Blacksburg from locations around town to two large parking areas ont the Virginia Tech campus and back. The locations of the signs can be seen at the marked locations on the map to the right.
